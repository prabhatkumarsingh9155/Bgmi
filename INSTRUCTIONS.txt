Box-based UI has been added to Teams section!

When Group Active is ON:
- Teams are displayed in separate colored boxes
- 🟣 GROUP A (purple box)
- 🟢 GROUP B (green box)  
- 📋 UNASSIGNED TEAMS (gray box)

Each team card shows:
- Slot number (colored by group)
- Team name
- Phone number
- Status badge
- Action buttons (APPROVE, ASSIGN/REMOVE SLOT, DELETE)

When Group Active is OFF:
- Shows traditional table view
- All teams in one list
